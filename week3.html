U.S. History - Week 3: Advanced Practice

Daily Structure

  1. Review: Key concepts introduction and recap.
  2. Instruction: Guided lesson for new or complex material.
  3. Practice: Application through exercises and short quizzes.
  4. Feedback: Identify and correct mistakes with explanations.

Example Objectives

  • Monday: Topic 1 review + diagnostic quiz
  • Tuesday: Topic 2 instruction + practice
  • Wednesday: Topic 3 instruction + quiz
  • Thursday: Review incorrect answers + targeted reteach
  • Friday: Weekly assessment + open Q&A
